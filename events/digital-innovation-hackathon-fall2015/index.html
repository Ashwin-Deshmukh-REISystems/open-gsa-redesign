Potential Projects:

Green House Gas (GHG) Reduction Visualization
How does increasing an agency's use of alternative vehicles such as diesel-powered, hybrid, and electric vehicles rather than traditional gas-powered vehicles affect the level of greenhouse gas emissions over a period of time? Help GSA answer this question by working on this project. More information can be found in the project files on GitHub.

Data Center Consolidation Mashup and Tool
How can multiple stakeholders including the White House, Congress, government agencies, and non-profit organizations come to better understand data center cost and utilization? How can GSA move from manual data center data collection and analysis to an automatic process assisting with inventory and cost accounting? Help GSA answer these questions by working on this project. More information can be found in the project files on GitHub.

Travel Challenge 2.0 Data Analysis and Mashup
How can federal government travel managers, CFOs, and other agency officials utilize travel data including airfare and hotel data to avoid travel costs governmentwide to employees and taxpayers? Help GSA answer this question by working on this project. More information can be found in the project files on GitHub.
